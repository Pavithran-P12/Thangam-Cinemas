About Thangam Cinemas

Thangam Cinemas started entertaining people since 1983. Having serviced for so many years, Thangam Cinemas has attained its pride and dignity by maintaining a clean, high-class, hygienic, audience-oriented cinema hall.

By 2015, Thangam Cinemas became the FIRST screen in Pollachi to feature multiplex interior with high acoustic soundproofing, state-of-the-art HARKNESS 2.4 GAIN SILVER SCREEN, imported 2K projector with high lumens for ultra-bright cinema with 3D, and DOLBY 7.1 surround sound system.
